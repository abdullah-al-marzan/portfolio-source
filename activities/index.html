Beyond the Lab: A Passion for Creativity and Community

While my professional life is dedicated to the intricacies of molecular biology and computational science, I believe that a well-rounded life is enriched by a deep engagement with the arts, education, and community. These activities not only provide a creative outlet but also enhance the skills of communication, leadership, and connection that are vital in any field.

Stage & Performance

My involvement in stage performances has been a significant part of my life, allowing me to express creativity and connect with audiences. From acting in plays to participating in dance performances, these experiences have honed my public speaking skills and boosted my confidence.



Couple Dance

Couple dance is a beautiful form of expression that combines rhythm, movement, and connection. In this video, I perform a couple dance with my partner, showcasing the joy and artistry of this dance style. The performance is not just about the steps; it's about the chemistry and communication between dancers, creating a captivating experience for the audience.



Participating in couple dance has taught me the importance of teamwork and trust. It has also enhanced my physical coordination and rhythm, skills that are beneficial in both dance and scientific research. I hope you enjoy the performance as much as we enjoyed creating it!



Puthi Recitation

Puthi recitation is a traditional form of storytelling and performance that has deep cultural roots. It involves reciting poetic texts, often with musical accompaniment, and is a way to preserve and share cultural narratives. In this video, I perform a Puthi recitation, showcasing the beauty of this art form. The performance is not just about the words; it's about the emotion and connection with the audience, bringing stories to life through voice and expression.



Participating in Puthi recitation has taught me the importance of cultural heritage and the power of storytelling. It has also enhanced my public speaking skills, allowing me to engage with audiences in a meaningful way. Enjoy the performance, and I hope it inspires you to explore the rich traditions of storytelling and performance in your own culture.


Drama Performance

Drama is a powerful medium for storytelling and expression. Participating in drama has taught me the importance of empathy and understanding different perspectives. It has also enhanced my ability to communicate effectively and connect with audiences on an emotional level.



In this video, I participated in a drama performance that humorously spoofs an advertisement, showcasing our creativity and comedic timing. The performance is a light-hearted take on the world of advertising, highlighting the absurdities and quirks of marketing.

Participating in this drama has been a fun and engaging experience, allowing me to explore the lighter side of performance art. It has also enhanced my ability to think creatively and work collaboratively with others to bring a shared vision to life. I hope you enjoy the humor and creativity in this performance!

Visual Arts & Drawing

Art is a powerful form of expression that transcends boundaries and connects people. My passion for drawing and visual arts has allowed me to explore my creativity and share my perspective with the world. In this section, I showcase some of my drawing tutorials, where I share techniques and tips for aspiring artists.

Drawing is not just about creating images; it's about capturing emotions, telling stories, and expressing ideas. Through my tutorials, I aim to inspire others to pick up a pencil and start their own artistic journey.

Education & Mentorship

I am passionate about teaching and making complex scientific concepts accessible to students. This video is an example of one of my chemistry tutorials, where I break down fundamental topics to help students build a strong foundation in the sciences.



In this tutorial, I explain key concepts in chemistry, using clear examples and engaging visuals to make the material more relatable. My goal is to demystify science and encourage students to explore their curiosity.

My approach to education emphasizes clarity, engagement, and practical applications. I believe that education is not just about imparting knowledge but also about inspiring curiosity and a love for learning. Through my tutorials, I aim to make science approachable and enjoyable for all learners.

Gaming & Community Moderation

As a community moderator for a video game, I learned valuable skills in communication, conflict resolution, and community management. I also enjoy creating helpful content, like this tutorial video, to assist other players and foster a positive and collaborative gaming environment.



In this video, I share tips and strategies for a popular game, helping players improve their skills and enjoy the game more fully. My goal is to create a supportive community where players can learn from each other and share their experiences.

Being a community moderator has taught me the importance of fostering a positive environment, where players can feel safe and supported. I strive to create content that not only entertains but also educates and empowers players to reach their full potential in the gaming world.
